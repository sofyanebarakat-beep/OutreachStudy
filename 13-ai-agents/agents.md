# AI Agents

## Research Agent
Finds schools, programs, costs, deadlines, and competitor pages.

## SEO Agent
Creates keyword lists, page briefs, meta titles, FAQs, and blog outlines.

## Content Agent
Creates reels scripts, captions, blog drafts, email copy, and ad copy.

## Lead Qualification Agent
Reviews lead answers and marks hot/warm/cold.

## Follow-up Agent
Suggests WhatsApp/email follow-up messages based on CRM stage.

## Analytics Agent
Reviews KPIs weekly and suggests what to improve.

## Landing Page Agent
Creates landing page copy and A/B test ideas.

# Study in Malta Marketing OS

A VS Code-ready folder to organize marketing, lead generation, tracking, analytics, content, sales follow-up, and AI agents for an app/business helping students study in Malta.

## Main goal
Generate qualified leads from students interested in studying in Malta and convert them into consultations/applications.

## Suggested workflow
1. Define offer and target audience.
2. Build website and landing pages.
3. Set tracking before launching campaigns.
4. Publish SEO/content assets.
5. Launch paid and organic campaigns.
6. Capture leads into CRM.
7. Follow up by WhatsApp/email/call.
8. Review KPIs weekly.
9. Improve conversion using experiments.

## Folder map
- `00-overview`: strategy, business goals, market notes
- `01-brand-and-offer`: positioning, services, student personas
- `02-website`: website structure and copy checklist
- `03-landing-pages`: page templates for campaigns
- `04-seo`: keywords, blog ideas, on-page SEO
- `05-content-marketing`: content calendar and ideas
- `06-paid-ads`: Meta/Google/TikTok campaign plans
- `07-social-media`: social strategy and scripts
- `08-email-whatsapp-crm`: follow-up messages and CRM stages
- `09-lead-capture-forms`: lead form fields and qualification
- `10-kpi-dashboard`: KPI tracker
- `11-tracking-analytics`: GA4, pixels, UTMs, events
- `12-sales-follow-up`: call scripts and pipeline
- `13-ai-agents`: agent roles and prompts
- `14-skills-and-team`: required skills and responsibilities
- `15-templates`: reusable templates
- `16-experiments`: A/B tests and growth experiments
- `17-assets`: place logos, images, videos, brochures here

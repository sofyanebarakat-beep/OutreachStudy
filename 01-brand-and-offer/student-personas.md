# Student Personas

## Persona 1: English Course Student
Needs: affordable English course, visa help, accommodation.
Pain points: unclear prices, fear of visa rejection, no local support.

## Persona 2: Higher Education Student
Needs: diploma/degree options, entry requirements, application support.
Pain points: not knowing which school is recognized, deadlines, documents.

## Persona 3: Parent
Needs: safety, cost clarity, accommodation, student support.
Pain points: trust, payment security, student wellbeing.

## Persona 4: Referral Agent
Needs: fast communication, commission clarity, school options.
Pain points: slow updates, unclear process.

## 14. CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)

**Formula:** `Total Marketing Spend ÷ Total Enrolled Students`

| Scenario | CAC | Interpretation |
|----------|-----|---------------|
| CAC < €200 | Excellent | Scale aggressively |
| CAC €200–€350 | Good | Maintain and optimize |
| CAC €350–€500 | Acceptable | Early stage OK, improve |
| CAC €500–€700 | Warning | Review funnel — fix WhatsApp/application drop-off |
| CAC > €700 | Critical | Pause paid spend, fix conversion |

| Channel | Spend | Enrolled | CAC |
|---------|-------|---------|-----|

## 16. ROAS Tracking Structure

**Formula:** `Revenue from Enrolled Students ÷ Total Ad Spend`

| ROAS Level | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| > 5x | Scale budget +50%, expand to new countries |
| 3–5x | Scale budget +20–30%, maintain strategy |
| 2–3x | Optimize — fix weakest funnel stage |
| 1.5–2x | Warning — review creative + audience |
| < 1.5x | Pause and diagnose — do not increase spend |

**Attribution Note:** Use 7-day click / 1-day view attribution on Meta. Last-click with UTM in GA4 as primary source of truth for registration attribution.

## ATTRIBUTION MODEL

**Recommended: Last Click (UTM) + First Touch tracking**

| Model | When to Use |
|-------|-------------|
| Last Click (GA4 primary) | Credit the channel that drove the final registration |
| First Touch (GA4 secondary) | Understand what introduced the student originally |
| Time Decay | For long sales cycles (application stage analysis) |

```
utm_source: meta / tiktok / google / instagram / whatsapp / organic / email
utm_medium: paid_social / cpc / seo / social / referral
utm_campaign: [campaign name — match naming convention]
utm_content: [ad name / creative name]
```

### Meta Ads
```
CAMPAIGN: META_[OBJECTIVE]_[AUDIENCE]_[COUNTRY]_[YYYYMM]
AD SET:   META_[Campaign]_[AudienceType]_[Age-Gender]_[Placement]
AD:       META_[Campaign]_[Format]_[Hook]_[Version]

META_LEADS_StudentProf_GH-NG_202505
```

```
CAMPAIGN: TT_[OBJECTIVE]_[COUNTRY]_[YYYYMM]
AD GROUP: TT_[Campaign]_[AudienceType]_[Age]
```

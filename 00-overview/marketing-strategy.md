# Marketing Strategy

## Objective
Get qualified leads for students who want to study in Malta.

## Target audiences
- International students looking for English language courses
- Students looking for diploma, bachelor, or master programs
- Parents who help pay for education
- Agents/partners who refer students

## Core offer
Free consultation to help students choose a Malta school/program, understand visa steps, costs, accommodation, and application requirements.

## Main channels
- SEO website
- Landing pages
- Google Search Ads
- Meta Ads
- TikTok/Reels organic content
- WhatsApp follow-up
- Email nurture
- Referral partners

## Funnel
Traffic → Landing page → Lead form/WhatsApp → Qualification → Consultation → Application → Enrollment

## Page goal
Convert ad/search/social traffic into leads.

## Structure
1. Headline: Study in Malta with Free Expert Guidance
2. Subheadline: Get help choosing your course, preparing documents, and planning your move.
3. CTA: Check Your Study Options
4. Benefits
5. Program types
6. Cost/visa summary
7. Testimonials
8. Lead form
9. FAQ
10. Final CTA

## Lead form fields
- Full name
- Email
- WhatsApp number
- Country
- Desired course
- When do you want to start?
- Budget range
- Visa support needed?

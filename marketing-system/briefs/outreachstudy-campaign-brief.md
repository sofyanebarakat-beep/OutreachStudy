# OutreachStudy Campaign Brief

## Business
OutreachStudy

## Goal
Generate student leads for study programs in Malta.

## Target Countries
- Morocco
- Algeria
- Tunisia

## Monthly Ad Budget
$1,000/month

## Primary Conversion Goals
- Lead form submission
- WhatsApp message
- Free consultation booking

## Target Audience
International students from Morocco, Algeria, and Tunisia who are interested in studying in Malta, learning English, applying for education programs, or getting support with student visa preparation.

## Core Offer Angles
- Study in Malta with guidance
- Student visa support
- Affordable study abroad option
- English-speaking destination
- Free consultation
- Help choosing schools/programs

## Main Channels
- Google Search Ads
- Meta Ads
- SEO content
- Instagram outreach
- TikTok content and replies
- WhatsApp follow-up

## Default Budget Allocation
- $500 High-intent Google Search campaigns
- $250 Competitor and comparison campaigns
- $150 Retargeting campaigns
- $100 Testing new creatives, countries, and audiences


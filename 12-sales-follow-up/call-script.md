# Sales Call Script

Hi [Name], I saw you are interested in studying in Malta. I’ll ask a few questions so I can recommend the best options.

- What do you want to study?
- When do you want to start?
- What is your current education level?
- What is your budget?
- Do you need accommodation?
- Do you need visa support?
- Have you applied anywhere before?
